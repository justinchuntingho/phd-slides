Situational Ethics

Expectation and Intention of the Individuals

Example: British Sociological Association

Officers arrested a 23-year-old man at his home, saying he had “incited others on an online forum to join the unlawful assembly in Mong Kok, to charge at police and to paralyze the railways.”

Police spokesman:
“I stress, inciting others to commit criminal acts on the Internet is illegal”

If this is what the individuals expected and intented, shall we honour their intention?


Are we doing the "dirty work" for the non-democratic regime?
